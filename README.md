# School-Based Medicaid: Audit Findings Data

Public data supporting reporting on school-based Medicaid claiming and contingency-fee consultants, prepared for Open the Books.

## The file

`DATA_SchoolMedicaid_PCG_AuditFindings_2026.xlsx` contains five tabs:

1. **Findings by Report**: five HHS-OIG audits (PA, NY, NJ, MI, plus a 10-state systemic review), with flagged dollar amounts, what auditors found, and current recommendation status.
2. 2. **Recommendations**: all 18 recommendations with amounts, statuses, and links to the OIG Recommendation Tracker.
   3. 3. **Per-Household Math**: flagged dollars divided by household counts (formulas live; denominators marked for verification against FRED and Census ACS).
      4. 4. **Timeline**: 2005 GAO warning through the active OIG Work Plan item (W-00-24-31529).
         5. 5. **Notes & Sources**: caveats and a source URL for every figure.
           
            6. ## Important caveats
           
            7. Improper or unsupported payments are not proven fraud. The figures are auditor-questioned costs and recommended refunds, not adjudicated losses. The states dispute the findings, and no fraud has been alleged against Public Consulting Group in any of the cited audits. The 2022 DOJ settlement resolved common-law claims with no admission of liability.
